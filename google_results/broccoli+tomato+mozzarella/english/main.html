Purple sprouting broccoli, mozzarella and tomato bake
  1. 2 tbsp olive oil.
  2. 1 small leek, sliced.
  3. 350-400g purple sprouting broccoli.
  4. 200g cherry tomatoes, halved.
  5. 200g mozzarella, roughly torn.
  6. 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped.
  7. 1 tbsp fresh marjoram or oregano leaves (or use 1 tsp dried)
  8. 2 tbsp breadcrumbs.
