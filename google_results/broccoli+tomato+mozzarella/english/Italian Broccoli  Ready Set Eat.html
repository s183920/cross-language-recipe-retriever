Italian Broccoli

Broccoli and tomato recipe combining frozen broccoli with Italian flavored tomatoes and finished with Italian blend cheese

Minutes Prep Time: 10

Minutes Total Time: 15

Servings: 6

Difficulty: easy

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 bag (14.4 oz each) Birds Eye® Tender Broccoli Cuts

  • 3 tablespoons water

  • 1 can (14.5 oz each) Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes with Basil, Garlic and Oregano, drained

  • 1/2 cup finely shredded Italian blend or mozzarella cheese

Nutrition Information

62 calories, 6g Carbs

Directions

Directions

  • Step one

    Place broccoli and water in medium skillet; cover. Cook over high heat 5 minutes or until broccoli is tender and water is almost evaporated.

  • Step two

    Add drained tomatoes; cook 2 to 3 minutes more or until hot. Sprinkle with cheese.

Tips

  • For gluten free preparation, confirm all recipe ingredients are gluten free by reading product labels each time you make this recipe.
